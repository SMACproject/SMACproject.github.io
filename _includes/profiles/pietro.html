Pietro Valdastri Principal Investigator

Pietro strongly believes that research in engineering must reach the end user to have an impact on society. He also has first-hand experience in start-up business creation. In 2009, he launched Winmedical srl, a med-tech company proposing a patented wearable modular system for continuous monitoring of physiological parameters. The company has 25 employees and is negotiating a potential acquisition with one of the worldwide leaders in medical device manufacturing.
